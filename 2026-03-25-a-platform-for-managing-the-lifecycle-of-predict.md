# Red Hat OpenShift AI

            - Date: 2026-03-25
            - Summary: A platform for managing the lifecycle of predictive and generative AI models across hybrid cloud environments.

            ## Notes
            Red Hat OpenShift AI is a platform designed to manage the entire lifecycle of predictive and generative AI (genAI) models. It enables organizations to deploy and manage AI/ML models at scale across hybrid cloud environments. The platform provides access to the latest AI/ML innovations and support, accelerating the adoption of generative AI.
