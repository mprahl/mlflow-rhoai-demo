# Red Hat OpenShift AI Overview

- Date: 2026-03-26
- Summary: Red Hat OpenShift AI is a platform for managing the lifecycle of AI/ML models across hybrid cloud environments.

## Notes
Red Hat OpenShift AI is a hybrid AI and MLOps platform built on Red Hat OpenShift. It provides tools for managing the full lifecycle of predictive and generative AI (genAI) models, from experimentation and training to serving and monitoring. It operates at scale across hybrid cloud environments, including on-site, public cloud, and edge deployments. The platform simplifies the delivery of AI solutions by offering self-service access to collaborative workflows, intensive computation power (GPUs), and streamlined operations.
